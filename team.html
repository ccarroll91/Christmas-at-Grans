The team

Michael Brennan, the author

Michael Brennan is a marketer and media creator from England. Having worked for companies such as Heineken, and creating magic for people of all ages as a former Cast Member for the Walk Disney company, Michael knows his way around a story.

Conor Carroll, the artist

Conor Carroll is an artist, educator and software developer from Ireland. With his artistic work focusing primarily on illustrations and comics, the opportunity to make a Michael Brennan story come to life was never going to be scoffed at.

Christmas at Gran's was written by Michael Brennan at the age of 10, and adapted into this digital story by Conor Carroll. It is with deep regret that Conor discovered that Bonzo did not, in fact, exist, and was a figment of Brennan's imagination for the sake of the poem. As Michael's brother Tom put it, he took "a bit of creative licence there". We hope that you love him all the same.
